Driving Innovation in the Mining Sector

Unearthed is a unique 54-hour long event focused on the mining sector. Software developers, designers, and industry insiders will come together to develop prototype solutions to mining sector problems. Unearthed participants will have a chance to work on proprietary industry data as well as data from our government partners.


Register to Attend

Cash and Glory

Thousands of dollars of cash prizes and other awards up for grabs.

Deep Industry Data

2 years of Gold Fields transport and logistics data. Geospacial data from Landgate and geological data from Dept of Mines and Petroleum. What will you build?

Experts on Hand

Collaborate with subject matter experts from industry. Insights from the field will help you build a solution with real impact.

 

Why innovation in mining?

David Peever

Managing Director / Rio Tinto Australia / 18 September 2012

"We need to innovate and be encouraged to do so. As a nation, we can’t afford complacency."

Productivity in the mining sector has dropped. To stay competitive internationally, it will have to innovate. Come be a part of that innovation. Help us build a sustaiable tech startup ecosystem in Australia. Want to learn more about how we can create Australian jobs and ensure the competitiveness of Australian industry? Who Should Participate?

Developers

Developer tickets are for those who will be active participants in the hackathon. If you are a coder, designer, or data guru keen to get building, this event is for you. If you are none technical we recommend coming along as an event observer.

Observers

Want to come along to watch the magic as it happens? The prime time for observers will be Friday night during the kick off event and Sunday evening for the presentations and prizes.
