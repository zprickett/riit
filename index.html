Driving Innovation in the Mining Sector

Unearthed is a unique 54-hour long event focused on the mining sector. Software developers, designers, and industry insiders will come together to develop prototype solutions to mining sector problems. Unearthed participants will have a chance to work on proprietary industry data as well as data from our government partners. Unearthed is organized by the Resource Innovation through Information Technology (RIIT) group.


The event for 2014 is over and was a resounding success. For a quick recap of the event head to the West "Thinking Big to Unearthed Ideas" for the story

Prizes

2014 Unearthed Results Winners below. Selected from the 18 participating teams


1st Prize

Applied Mathematics Inc.

Pre-crusher large bolder detection with off the shelf accelerometers and real time mathematical analysis


2nd Prize

PIT-IQ

iPad app which gives mine grade controllers mobile access to graded block information. Blocks are visualised on a map, with key data such as grade, tonnage, mineral composition and dug date, just a tap away.


Landgate SLIP Prize

The Froys

Interactive map of ore transport across the mine site. Our tool makes it easier for management to keep tabs on the day's movements, compare them with historical records, and reconcile their forecast totals -- from any device, anywhere

Who Should Participate?

Developers

Developer tickets are for those who will be active participants in the hackathon. If you are a coder, designer, or data guru keen to get building, this event is for you. If you are nontechnical we recommend coming along as an event observer.

Observers

Want to come along to watch the magic as it happens? The prime time for observers will be Friday night during the kick off event and Sunday evening for the presentations and prizes.
